# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the EuroleagueTech Cloud Platform community will experience an environment free from harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Be Respectful**: Value different opinions and experiences
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome contributors from all backgrounds
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together constructively
- **Be Patient**: Help others learn and grow
- **Be Professional**: Keep discussions focused and on-topic
- **Be Accountable**: Take responsibility for your words and actions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Offensive comments regarding identity, background, or beliefs
- Unwelcome sexual advances or comments
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Appropriate action will be taken in response to confirmed violations, which may include:

- Warnings
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all EuroleagueTech community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Pull requests and issues
- Project communication channels
- Official events and gatherings
