A knowledge platform for understanding sports technology in professional basketball

OUR PURPOSE

Euroleague Tech Hub is a comprehensive reference platform designed to map and understand the sports technology ecosystem in Euroleague Basketball. We provide structured, verified data about the vendors and solutions that power performance analytics, video analysis, recovery, fan engagement, and more across professional basketball teams.

Whether you're a sports technology professional researching the market, a team executive evaluating vendor options, or simply curious about how technology shapes modern basketball, this platform serves as your central knowledge hub.

Key Objectives:

  • ▸ Catalog technology vendors operating in professional basketball
  • ▸ Document confirmed technology partnerships with Euroleague teams
  • ▸ Categorize solutions by function (performance, video, recovery, fan engagement, etc.)
  • ▸ Provide a searchable, filterable directory for quick reference
  • ▸ Enable comparison of technology stacks across teams and vendors
